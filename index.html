About Me

I'm a
self-taught photographer with a passion for travel and design

I was born in Cape Town, South Africa and moved around a lot as a kid. To keep me distracted, my parents gave me a camera. Since then, I look at the world through a lens. I've visited over 30 countries and taken almost one quarter of a million photographs, and I feel like I'm just getting started. From landscapes to personal portraits and exciting scenes, I love being able to capture a moment and share it with others. Join me on my journey!
